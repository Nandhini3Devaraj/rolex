## 🎯 How to Use

### Adding Product with Image (3 Easy Steps)

#### Step 1: Copy Image URL
1. Go to https://unsplash.com (or any website)
2. Search for product (e.g., "smartphone", "watch", "shoes")
3. Right-click on image
4. Click "Copy Image Address"
5. ✅ URL copied!

#### Step 2: Open Add Product Form
1. Click "Add" button on products page
2. Look for purple-bordered section (impossible to miss!)
3. Find "🖼️ PRODUCT IMAGE URL" field

#### Step 3: Paste & Create
1. Paste URL into image field
2. Fill other fields (name, description, category, price, stock)
3. Click "✨ Create Product"
4. ✅ Product appears with your image!
